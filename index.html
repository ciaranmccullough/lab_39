About Us

We offer a range of innovative solutions to meet the demands of today's dental market. We specialise in crown and bridge work, and work with only the best materials including semi-precious metals, noble alloys, zirconia, composities and Emax. Our implant expertise covers Straumman, Astra and Nobel Biocare amongst other newer brands, and we are set up with a 3D printer, scanner and digital workflow ready to receive digital files.

We now receive digital impressions for 3Shape TRIOS and Itero 3D! Connect with us via email and the contact details provided below.
